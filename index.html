Ideally, I studied Photoshop, Adobe Effects/VFX, I also do 3D modeling, and I'm learning to integrate 3D models into the site, I studied HTML5, CSS3, SASS (with BEM template and 7-1, Bootstrap), basic knowledge of DOM, JAVASCRIPT (with OOP and basic CANVAS), as well as the basics of React. and WordPress.
